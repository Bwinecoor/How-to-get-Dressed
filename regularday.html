First, you want to get a layout of all of your clothes. Grab your socks, underwear/boxers, a shirt, some pants, and a hoodie and or jacket of your choice.
Second, when you want to get dressed for a regular day, depending on the temperature you can choose to wear a short or long sleeve shirt! If it is cold or windy you should wear a long sleeve shirt and if it is warm you should wear a t-shirt so you are not too hot when outside. Also, if it is cold outside you should wear long pants, like jeans or sweatpants. If it is hot or warm outside it's recommended you wear shorts. If it is cold, wear a jacket and/or a hoodie, if it is warm you dont need to wear one.
Third, make sure that you outfit looks good on you and make sure it has some style to it. If you wear a dark colored shirt wear dark colored pants, and if you wear a light colored shirt wear light covered pants, and vice versa.
